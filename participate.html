Participate

Please consider this an invitation to any academics with proposals regarding scaling the Bitcoin network. If you have a proposal, we would like to have you make a 20-30 min talk about it. We would prefer proposals which have some experimental supporting data to back up the proposal.

If you are interested in making a presentation, please contact contact@onchainscaling.com, and please include a copy or link to an abstract of your research paper or your presentation topic. The conference is tentatively scheduled to be 27th-28th of May. If you have scheduling concerns or special considerations, please let us know.

Topics of interest may include:

  • On-Chain Scaling solutions for Bitcoin - i.e. bandwidth, txn/s, or users.
  • Concerns about Hard Forks and Soft Forks (moral hazard, network stability, attacks)
  • Solutions that will make Hard forks transparent and painless for users
  • Governance and Bitcoin election processes
  • Defense against adversaries with deep pockets and bad faith
  • Fees market vs Block capacity
  • Decentralization vs Network effect
  • Measuring network security in terms of costs to corruption
  • Free PoW elections vs meta-consensus
  • Contentious hard forks dangerous vs natural
