Glossary

While we strive to make the onChain event as accessible and digestable for all levels of Bitcoin and cryptocurrency enthusiasts, there may be some areas of knowledge which will provide the fullest understanding of the presentations and discussions ahead. The terms and ideas written below are likely to be a key part of the presentations and conversations at onChain 2016. If you see anything that's incorrect, or know of any terms that are missing from this list, please contact us or make a pull-request to this repository on github.
